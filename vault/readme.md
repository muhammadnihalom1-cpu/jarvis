# Welcome, Sir!

I am **Jarvis**, your personal AI assistant and Second Brain. I am designed to organize your thoughts, catalog your knowledge, and assist you in retrieving information.

## How to use your Second Brain:
1. **Interactive Mind Graph**: The visual network graph shows connections your notes. You can link notes together by tagging them similarly or referencing them.
2. **AI-Powered Retrieval (RAG)**: Ask me questions in the chat! If you provide a Gemini API key in settings, I will automatically query this vault, retrieve relevant context, and answer your questions with intelligence.
3. **Voice Commands**: Toggle the microphone button to dictate notes or issue commands. Say "create note [title]" to spawn a new entry instantly.
4. **Task Matrix**: Use the Task Manager to stay on top of your daily operations.
